Syn-Aesthetic
Click Around to Find Color

Synesthesia helps you see and navigate the world though color. Whether it is a color triggered by a sound, name or some food, it adds a layer of experience. As someone who has synesthesia, I wanted to help people experience this website the way I experience the world- by navigating though color. The more you interact with the website, the more color you will find. P.S. The piano tiles below take you to the color they display!
